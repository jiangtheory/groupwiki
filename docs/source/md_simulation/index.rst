Molecular Dynamics Simulation: Polymeric Materials with LAMMPS
==============================================================

Welcome to the Molecular Dynamics Simulation section of our research group wiki.


Overview
--------

Molecular Dynamics (MD) is a computational technique that simulates the motion of atoms and molecules in a system. Our group specializes in MD simulations of polymeric materials using LAMMPS:

- Polymer chain dynamics and conformations
- Mechanical properties and viscoelasticity
- Polymer blends and miscibility
- Glass transition temperature (Tg)
- Cross-linking and network formation
- Polymer-nanoparticle composites
- Surface properties and interfacial phenomena
