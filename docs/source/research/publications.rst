Publication Format
------------------

**Journal Articles**

   Author(s). "Title of article". *Journal Name*, Year, Volume(Issue), pages. DOI.

**Conference Proceedings**

   Author(s). "Title". Proceedings of Conference, Year, pages. DOI/URL.

**Preprints**

   Author(s). "Title". arXiv:XXXX.XXXXX, Year.

**Technical Reports**

   Author(s). "Title". Technical Report, Institution, Year.

Contributing to Publications
-----------------------------

Research workflow:

1. **Conduct Research**: Execute project
2. **Write Manuscript**: Draft paper
3. **Internal Review**: Group feedback
4. **Revisions**: Improve based on feedback
5. **Submission**: To journal or conference
6. **Peer Review**: Editor and reviewers
7. **Publication**: Published work
8. **Dissemination**: Share widely

See :doc:`../collaboration/guidelines` for writing standards.
