About Our Research Group
=========================

Our Mission
-----------

We are a multidisciplinary research group dedicated to advancing knowledge in molecular dynamics simulation, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. Our research bridges computational chemistry, statistical physics, and AI to solve complex scientific problems.


Research Focus Areas
--------------------

**Molecular Dynamics Simulation**
   Studying molecular behavior, protein folding, drug design, and materials science through computational simulation.

**Machine Learning**
   Developing ML models for scientific data analysis, prediction, and pattern recognition in computational research.

**Artificial Intelligence**
   Applying AI techniques to accelerate discovery, automate analysis, and enhance research workflows.


Group Structure
---------------

Our group is organized around collaborative research projects that often span multiple areas:

- Core research faculty and researchers
- Graduate students and postdoctoral fellows
- undergraduate researchers and interns
- Visiting scholars and collaborators


Core Values
-----------

- **Rigor**: High standards for computational methods and validation
- **Collaboration**: Sharing knowledge and cross-disciplinary work
- **Open Science**: Transparency in methods and results
- **Innovation**: Pushing boundaries of what's computationally possible
- **Support**: Mentoring and helping all group members succeed


Collaboration & Partnerships
-----------------------------

We actively collaborate with:

- Other research groups in our institution
- National labs and research centers
- International computational science communities
- Industry partners in materials science and pharmaceutical research
